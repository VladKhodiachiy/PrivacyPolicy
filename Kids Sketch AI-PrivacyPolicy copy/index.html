5. WHAT FACE OR IMAGE DATA DO WE COLLECT?

In Short: We do not collect or store your face data permanently. We only temporarily process the image of your child's drawing to generate a stylized result using AI.

To generate a realistic or artistic image from your child's drawing, your uploaded photo is first sent to our backend servers hosted on the Google Cloud Platform (USA) for temporary processing. From there, it is securely sent via API to AI services Open AI where the image is transformed based on your selected style. Your original uploaded image may contain drawings that include human-like faces, but we do not use or extract any actual face recognition data, nor do we attempt to identify or analyze personal facial features.

Your original uploaded image may contain drawings that include human-like faces. However, we do not use, analyze, or extract any real face recognition data from these images, nor do we attempt to identify or profile any individuals. These are treated solely as artistic content, not biometric data.

Here’s how we handle your data:

  • Your uploaded drawing is stored temporarily, only for the time it takes to complete the image generation process.
  • Once the transformation is finished, the original uploaded photo is automatically deleted from both our servers and any third-party AI providers.
  • The generated image is stored securely on our server for up to 48 hours to give you time to access and download it. After 48 hours, it is permanently deleted and cannot be retrieved.
  • Credits used to generate images are non-refundable and will be deducted even if the final result is not accessed within the 48-hour window.

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your uploaded drawings or generated results to any third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, or analytics platforms. We also do not use your images for training or improving any AI models.

Can backend services change over time?
Yes, the infrastructure or third-party services we use may change in the future. Any such changes will be transparently updated in this Privacy Policy.

Important Note: This app is intended for transforming children’s artwork only. We recommend that parents or guardians supervise image uploads to ensure that only drawings are submitted and that no personal photographs of real individuals are included.
