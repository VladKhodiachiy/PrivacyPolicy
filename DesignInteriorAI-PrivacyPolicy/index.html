5. DO WE COLLECT FACE DATA?

In Short: No. We do not collect, store, sell, or use face data — even if a face happens to appear in a room photo you upload.

Our app is designed to work with room photos only. When you upload a photo to generate a new interior design, the following process occurs:

  • Your photo is securely uploaded to our backend servers (currently hosted on Google Cloud Platform, USA).
  • The photo is sent via API to our AI service provider’s servers, where it is processed and transformed into a new interior design image.
  • During this process, your original photo is temporarily stored only as needed to complete the transformation.
  • Once the design image has been generated, your original photo — including any face data that may have appeared in the room photo — is permanently deleted from both Google Cloud Platform and the AI service provider’s servers.
  • The resulting design image is stored on our server for up to 48 hours so you can view and download it. After 48 hours, it is automatically and permanently deleted.

Please note that any credits used for generation are non-refundable, regardless of whether the final image is accessed before deletion.

We do not share, sell, or transfer your photos or any face data that may appear in them to third parties (including advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or AI training services). Your photos are never used to train artificial intelligence models.

We may update or change our backend server providers in the future. If this happens, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.
